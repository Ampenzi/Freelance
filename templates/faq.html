Orders Rules

  • Only Accept or Bid the orders that you can handle (Checking deadline and complexity)

  • Always read the instruction and ensure everything is clear. Never work on assignments with unclear instructions.

  • Always remember to check for order files and messages before starting the assignment.

  • Do not assign anyone the task. If you are busy just wait. You will get more orders when available.

  • Check the order well for the first 30 minutes and confirm that you can handle.

  • Reassigning after staying with the order for long will be fined.

  • Always request for deadline extension 4 hours prior to the deadline

  • Do not paraphrase the assignment from course hero or any other academic writing website. Doing so will attract a fine of not less than 3000.

  • Failed work will not be paid and may attract a fine or suspension.

Order Stages

  • Available:Means orders are available and you can place your bid

  • Applications: You placed your bid but not yet assigned. Don’t work on these orders.

  • Assigned: Means that you have been assigned and currently working on the tasks.

  • Editing: Orders are being reviewed by the editor

  • Completed: Orders were approved by the editors

  • Revision: Orders were sent to revision by either editor or the client (Work onrevisions asap to avoid getting the orders cancelled).

  • approved: Orders are approved and will be paid in the next payday.

Requirements for every work

  • Upload full completed work

  • Upload a copy of plagiarism report as well

  • Proper grammar and format

  • Consistent with references such as lectures and textbooks

  • Plagiarism rate not higher than 5%

  • Modify as customers ask (They pay the bill, just do what they want, it does not matter whether it is right or not)

  • If there is no special requirement, there should be at least 6 academic citation sources per thousand words, and use appropriate format, such as introduction, subtitle, summary.

  • Don’t exceed the number of pages / words indicated with more than 10%

Term of payment

  • We make payment twice a month.

  • We settle the orders done at least 14 days ago. (Orders marked as approved)

  • You can send invoice any time after the orders have been approved.

Sending Invoice

  • Log in to your account

  • Go to Financial overview or click you balance on top

  • Go to Accounts

  • Confirm payment details

  • Request invoice (the amount you wish to withdraw not necessarily full amount)

  • Send Invoice

  • To know that your invoice was sent successfully, your balance will reduce with the amount you sent.

Deduction

Due to quality purposes, the security of accounts and relationships with clients we make the following deductions.

If the following situations occur, we will deduct ksh 250:

  • *Plagiarism higher than 10%

  • Too many grammatical errors (No Grammarly was used)

  • Lack of proofreading the work

  • The requirements of assignment are not met

If orders are delayed, there will be different deductions depending on the length of the time

Delay Time Deduction
Within 1 hr 100 ksh

Within 6 hours 500 ksh
Longer than 6 hours 800 ksh

If you confirm the order but do not finish it or fail, additional 500 ksh will be deducted from your payment. Make sure you only accept orders you can handle well, and be responsible for every order.
